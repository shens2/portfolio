Context and challenge

While Word online automatically proofreads your document based on your default language, there's a huge pain point that was repeatedly brought up by our users - proofreading in multi-language document.

Currently, there's no way to change the proofread language for the whole document. You can change the proofreading language of whatever you highlight, but it gets reverted back to your default as soon as you move your IP.

It's also a real hassle for users to change their default language now because it applies to all Office apps, and it's tied to the UI language. They don't have a way to only change the default language for the app they are using.

Our goal is to allow users to seamlessly proofread their document in different languages, and set their app-level proofread language without affecting their UI language.
